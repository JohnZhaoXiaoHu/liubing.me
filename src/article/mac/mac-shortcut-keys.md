# Mac 常用快捷键整理

自从换了 Mac 笔记本后，Mac 上的快捷键老是会忘记需要去网上搜索，索性自己整理一下日常常用的快捷键，加深下印象，方便查找。以下内容整理自苹果官网[Mac 键盘快捷键](https://support.apple.com/zh-cn/HT201236)

<!-- more -->

## 常用快捷键

### 复制

Command ⌘ + V

<MacKeyboard :keys="['command', 'V']" />

### 剪切

<MacKeyboard :keys="['option', 'command', 'V']" />

### 打开文件

<MacKeyboard :keys="['command', 'O']" />

<MacKeyboard :keys="['command', 'delete']" />

<MacKeyboard :keys="['command', 'P']" />

### 存储文件

<MacKeyboard :keys="['command', 'S']" />

<MacKeyboard :keys="['command', 'T']" />

<MacKeyboard :keys="['command', 'W']" />

<MacKeyboard :keys="['option', 'command', 'W']" />

<MacKeyboard :keys="['option', 'command', 'esc']" />

<MacKeyboard :keys="['command', 'space']" />

<MacKeyboard :keys="['control', 'command', 'space']" />

### 全屏 App

Control ⌃ + Command ⌘ + F

<MacKeyboard :keys="['control', 'command', 'F']" />

### 切换应用

Command ⌘ + Tab

<MacKeyboard :keys="['command', 'tab']" />

<MacKeyboard :keys="['shift', 'command', '5']" />

<MacKeyboard :keys="['enter']" />

### 新建文件夹

<MacKeyboard :keys="['shift', 'command', 'N']" />
